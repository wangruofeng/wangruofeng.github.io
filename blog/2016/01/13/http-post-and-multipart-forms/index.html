request body like this

    --YOUR_BOUNDARY_STRING

    Content-Type: image/jpeg

    YOUR_IMAGE_DATA_GOES_HERE

    Content-Disposition: form-data; name="message"

    My first message
    --YOUR_BOUNDARY_STRING
    Content-Disposition: form-data; name="user"

    1
    --YOUR_BOUNDARY_STRING
